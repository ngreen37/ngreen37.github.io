A nearby island where chess is a footnote and Shogi is everything. Shogi Island operates by its own rules and its own language.

## Notable Features
- Located near both Checker Town and Chess City
- Shogi is the dominant game; chess is rarely played
- Everyone on the island speaks Japanese
- Has its own distinct culture, separate from the chess-and-checkers world of the mainland

## Role in the Story
Shogi Island exists at the edges of the story -- a reminder that the world is bigger than the chess-versus-checkers divide. Whether the narrator or Princess passes through here remains to be seen.
