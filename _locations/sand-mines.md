Hard labor at the far end of a bad road. The Sand Mines are one of the few employment options available to Checker Town citizens -- and one of the hardest to escape.

## Notable Features
- Workforce is made up almost entirely of Checker Town residents
- Run by corporate interests who maintain tight control over workers
- Workers are either physically constrained or find themselves unable to leave for other reasons
- Located too far from Checker Town for the narrator to stay close to Princess -- a significant factor in his calculus

## Mysteries
There are rumors of a portal -- called the *pu-ri-n-se-suuuu* portal -- that may connect the Sand Mines to somewhere else entirely. What lies on the other side is unknown.

## Role in the Story
The Sand Mines represent one of the worst-case outcomes for someone from Checker Town. The narrator may end up here. Whether he does -- and whether he can leave -- is part of what drives the story forward.
