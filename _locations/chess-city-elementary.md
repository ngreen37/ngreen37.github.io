A school inside Chess City where the narrator finds work -- first as a janitor, and eventually as a chess teacher.

## Notable Features
- Located in Chess City, which means the narrator must pass through the security checkpoint and get Tagged every single shift
- The job sits at an uncomfortable intersection: it is inside the city he wants to belong to, but accessed only as a worker
- The options facing the narrator are narrow: Chess City Elementary, college (which feels like a dead end), or the Sand Mines (which are too far from Princess)

## Role in the Story
Chess City Elementary is where the narrator's journey and his teaching life begin to converge. It is a place of contradiction -- close to the dream, but still at arm's length.
