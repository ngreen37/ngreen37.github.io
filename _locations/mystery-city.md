Nobody knows its real name. Nobody knows exactly where it is. But everyone has heard the rumors.

## What People Say
- It is the Capital of Chess -- the origin of everything
- The checker pieces that built Checker Town fell from somewhere, and this city is where they came from
- Details beyond that are scarce, disputed, or deliberately buried

## Role in the Story
The mystery city lurks at the edge of the story's mythology. It may be the answer to questions the characters don't yet know how to ask.
