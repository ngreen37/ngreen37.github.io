{% comment %} ── 2. PJCC DESCRIPTOR ───────────────────────────────── {% endcomment %}

Princess and the Journey to Chess City is an original animated cartoon series in development by McPuppy Studios. It follows Princess -- a loyal, chess-playing dog -- and the young man who raises her as they navigate life in Checker Town, a struggling community where checker pieces once fell from the sky, and chase a better life in Chess City across the sea.

This site documents the project as it's being built: the story, the characters, the locations, and everything in between. Lessons learned along the way get posted to the blog.
