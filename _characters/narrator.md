The story's guide and driving force. A young man from Checkers Town with big dreams, a strong work ethic, and a deep bond with his dog, Princess.

## Traits
- Male, between 14 and 21 years old
- Ambitious and hopeful, but grounded in the hard reality of Checkers Town
- Talks with his hands a lot
- Has a knack for getting people genuinely excited about things

## Background
He was born and raised in Checkers Town, a place where hope is in short supply and most people never leave. He has his sights set on Chess City -- not just as a destination, but as proof that a better life is possible. His path there is not straightforward: work, sacrifice, and eventually being sent away all stand between him and where he wants to go.

## Role in the Story
The narrator is the lens through which we experience Checkers Town, Chess City, and the world in between. His relationship with Princess is the emotional core of the series.
